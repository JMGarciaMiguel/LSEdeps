The verb particle relation _compound:prt_ is used for the particle element of a particle verb construction, usually in a construction calqued from Swedish.

~~~ sdparse
PÅ VÄG VARVA NER \n [I'm] on [my] way to wind down
compound(VARVA, NER)
~~~
