An adjectival modifier of a nominal is any adjectival phrase that serves
to modify the meaning of the nominal head.

~~~ sdparse
POSS1 EGEN SPRÅK VETA-INTE PRO1 \n I didn't know my own language
amod(SPRÅK, EGEN)
~~~
